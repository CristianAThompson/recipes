4 Eggs
7 Ounces White Sugar
6 Ounces Brown Sugar
5.75 Ounces Cocoa Powder
2.5 Ounces All Purpose Flour
1/2 Teaspoon Salt
2 Teaspoon Vanilla
8 Ounces Melted Butter

Combine Sugar and eggs and beat until lighter in color.

Add in Salt, Vanilla, Cocoa Powderm, Flour and Butter. Mix just until well
combined.

Place in oven pre-heated to 300 degrees farenheit.

Bake for 45 minutes or until thermometer reaches 195 degrees Farenheit.

Let cool and cut into desired size.
